Welcome to my blog

April 29, 2012 Posted by: Abel Maniquis

 

Hello. Welcome to my blog. This first post will be posted whenever I get the css to my liking. Before I continue. The original inspiration for this blog's design is from templated's keyboard template. I figured that creating a blog would be the perfect way to practice my design fundamentals. Yes, no wordpress for me, I plan on building this thing with pure HTML/CSS. That way, all the stuff on it belongs to me.

I will be hosting this site on Github Pages until the site gets big enough for me to justify having my own url. For the moment it's going to be me blogging my thoughts.
